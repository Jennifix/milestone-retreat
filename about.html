About Us

The Milestone Retreat is a non-religious center for those who wish to explore themselves and nature. Our goal is encouraging people to take a moment and leave the stressful civilization behind and focus on their own well-being. Living together at the retreat also promotes compassion and respect towards other people and beings.

The year was 1965 when our founder Auraride Spaceflow first began to build our cottage that later became a second home to many of our visitors. Spaceflow wanted to learn what it meant to be one with nature and share his experiences with his students. One of the students, Soulbeat Zendream, kept the cottage alive and developed a retreat.

What We Do

We offer courses in yoga, meditation, cooking and more as well as counseling and support for those who need it. The retreat runs on donations and our passionated volonteers work together to create a safe space for exploration and growth.

Other activities include growing their own vegetables, fishing on the river, camping and for the more adventurous there's canyoning or an initiation in rock-climbing.
